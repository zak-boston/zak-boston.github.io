**Problem**  
What is the sum of all odd fibonacci numbers less than `4000000`

**Solution (O(n))**
```python
x = 0
y = 1
z = 0
answer = 0
while z <= 4000000:
  if z%2==0:
    answer += z
  z = x + y
  x = y
  y = z
